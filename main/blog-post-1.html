About Online Classes

I used to wake up at 7 AM, eat breakfast, and attend my 8 AM class. Now that all classes have transitioned to an online format, I can work on assignments and study for exams when I feel more focused (which is around 2 AM)

Benefits of Online Classes


Flexible Schedule
I can study anytime, anywhere I want. I can even watch online lectures while running on the treadmill!


Low Costs
I used to live on-campus which means I don't have to pay for dorm and meal plan anymore. Last year I paid 12,000$ for it.


Avoid Commuting
Although school buses are available, I usually walk from class to class since it is a good exercise. With online classes, I don't have to worry about rain, snow, or 90°F heat in July.
